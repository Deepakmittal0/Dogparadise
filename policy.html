Privacy & Policy

At Puppies Paradise, we are committed to providing healthy, genuine breed puppies and ensuring complete customer satisfaction.

24 Hours Health Guarantee

Every puppy purchased from Puppies Paradise comes with a 24-hour health guarantee from the time of delivery. Customers are advised to have the puppy examined by a licensed veterinarian within this period.

Lifetime Breed Guarantee

We guarantee that the breed information provided at the time of purchase is accurate and authentic. This assurance remains valid throughout the puppy's lifetime.

Lifetime Free Support

Our team provides lifetime assistance regarding feeding, grooming, training, and general puppy care at no additional cost.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide proper nutrition and clean living conditions.
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedules.
  • Ensure regular veterinary checkups.
  • Provide a safe and caring environment for the puppy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our policies, please contact our support team for assistance.
